Refund Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Refund Policy explains how payments, cancellations and refunds are handled for TradeWise plans. By purchasing a plan, you agree to the terms below.

1. Nature of Our Service

TradeWise provides access to digital content — trade analysis, insights and market coverage delivered through our Telegram channel. Access is granted immediately once your payment is confirmed.

2. General Policy

Because our plans give instant access to digital content that cannot be returned, all payments are non-refundable once access has been provided. Please review the plan details and the risks involved before purchasing.

3. Exceptions

We may issue a refund at our discretion in cases such as:

  • A duplicate or accidental charge for the same plan.
  • A technical issue on our side that prevents you from accessing the channel and cannot be resolved within a reasonable time.
  • Any situation where a refund is required by applicable law.

4. Cancellations

You can stop using the service and leave the Telegram channel at any time. Cancelling stops any future renewals; it does not create a right to a refund for the current period already accessed.

5. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify under Section 3, contact us on Telegram within 7 days of the charge. Include your payment reference and a short description of the issue. We aim to review eligible requests within 5–7 business days.

6. Contact

For any billing or refund question, reach us on Telegram and we'll be glad to help.
